The Walk Home

She decided she would have to go home with no sign of her grandfather, a weird strom brewing, a sea creature, and POWers? She decided that tonight she should not be out. Something about the strangeness, and what happened down at the harbor? Was she still drunk from the night before? Maybe her dad had an explaination? She remembered stories of creatures back in the day, but nothing too specific. She walked eagerly on the cobble stones to get on the trek to her cliff-topped house, and swore she saw the constellation Cu run across the sky. Had she forgotten that this was the year of the hunt?
